"Stock Scraper"

Stock Scraper is a front to back data science project which utilizes essentially every major aspect of a typical data science lifecycle. It consists of everything from web scraping and data management to exploratory data analysis and machine learning. The end goal of this project is to construct a LTSM neural network model that uses both fundamental and technical indicators to forecast stock prices of interest.

"Find it HERE"

Find it HERE originated from taking on an NCR challenge at UGAHacks 6 and was designed to sovle some of NCR's biggest challenges while helping the local community. Due to the pandemic, a majority of retail shopping to migrate online, shutting down small and midsized, local businesses. This Chrome extension keeps shopping simple while serving as a guide to support your local community.
